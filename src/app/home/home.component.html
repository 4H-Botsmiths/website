About the Botsmiths

The Botsmiths is a team located in Snohomish County Washington and sponsored by the Snohomish County 4-H Technology Program. As a 4-H Technology Program, 4-H Clubs from across Snohomish County you are welcome to join the Botsmiths! The 4-H Technology Botsmiths offers opportunities for youth in First Robotics Competition, First Tech Challenge, First Lego League, and First Lego League Junior. We also run a fully online Minecraft group
